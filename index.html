Wear Today — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Wear Today ("the app") is a weather-based clothing recommendation app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

  • Location: The app requests access to your device's location (GPS / network) solely to fetch local weather conditions. Your location is sent directly to a public weather API (Open-Meteo) to retrieve weather data. It is never stored or shared by us.
  • Weather data: Current weather information is fetched from Open-Meteo, a free and open-source weather API. No personal information is included in these requests.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account or registration is required
  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is stored on our servers — we have none

Third-Party Services

The app uses Open-Meteo to retrieve weather data. Open-Meteo is an open-source project and does not require API keys or user identification. Please refer to their privacy policy for details on their data handling.

Permissions

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Used only to determine your current location for weather lookup. Never stored.
  • INTERNET: Required to fetch weather data from the Open-Meteo API.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
